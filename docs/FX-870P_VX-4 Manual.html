3formula function,
4data bank function,

6Although labels cannot be used, other dimension arrays, relatively powerful BASIC, which can use variable names of up to 255 characters,
7C language interpreter,
8The cash,

1. Basic operation

1-1. Replacing the battery

　The batteries used by FX-870P/VX-4 are:
    Operating Batteries : Single three-battery (American standard AA) × 4pcs
    Batteries for data storage : CR1220 × 1 ... (Note)
There are two types of batteries,Press the ON key ("BRK" key) but it does not start.Or, after pressing the ON key,When a low battery message appears,The replacement of the batteries will be required.
　As a precaution when replacing the battery,

  • When the operation battery and the data storage battery are removed at the same time, the data such as the program is not saved,
  • When the operation battery / data storage battery is removed at the same time, the P button on the back of the main unit and the ALL RESET button on the front of the main unit It is necessary to press sequentially with a thin stick of the tip, such as toothpicks.
and is raised,

Replacement of operating batteries (single-three batteries; AA batteries)
  1. Screw of the metal pig of the main body Three screws are turned with coins, etc., and the metal pig is removed.
  2. When you remove the metal pig, there is a slide switch engraved with ON, OFF on the back of the main body. Turn off that switch.
  3. Slide while holding down the battery slide pig ▼ and remove the battery slide pig.
  4. Take out the old battery and set four AA batteries according to the instructions on the imprint of the inner electrode.
  5. Bring the battery slide pig back to normal.
  6. Turn on the slide switch.
  7. Put on a metal pig and tighten three screws.

Replacing data storage batteries (CR1220)
　Since the life of the data storage battery is twenty-four months, it is necessary to replace it once every two years.
  1. Screw of the metal pig of the main body Three screws are turned with coins, etc., and the metal pig is removed.
  2. When you remove the metal pig, there is a slide switch engraved with ON, OFF on the back of the main body. Turn off that switch.
  3. Since the bottom of the round press plate of about 1 inch (2.54 cm) near the slide switch is the place where CR1220 is set, loosen the small screw that is fastened and remove the pressing plate.
  4. Set the battery by placing the + electrode of CR1220 on the upper side (side close to the presser when the presser is put on).
  5. Put on the press and tighten the small screws.
  6. Turn on the slide switch.
  7. Put on a metal pig and tighten three screws.

As a precaution when replacing a single battery (AA battery) or CR1220, leave the slide switch off during the replacement operation.

For example, if FX-870P, VX4 cannot start "BRK" key (ON key) successfully before replacing the battery, for example, for reasons such as not using it for a long time, Press the P button on the back of the main body and the ALL RESET button on the front of the main body with a thin stick on the tip like a toothpick. 1-2. Power ON/OFF and contrast adjustment

Power ON
　The red "BRK" key on the right side also serves as the ON key, so press this button. When it starts up normally, it enters CAL mode, and the cursor flashes and the input waiting state is reached.
If there is no reaction even if you press it, the possibilities other than the breakdown and the countermeasures are as follows.

  • It is working normally, but the contrast of the LCD is zero and the display is not visible. Adjust the LCD to the contrast.
  • The Low Battery continues to use, or the system is in some kind of runaway state. →Press the P button on the back of the main body with a thin stick on the tip such as toothpicks. If this is the case, the program and files may be safe. If you are worried, press the ALL RESET button to initialize the RAM.
  • The single three-battery for operation is out. → Replacing a single battery, and in some cases, a CR1220 for data storage is also performed.

13-6. About rounding treatment

　In FX-870P / VX4, it is calculated in CAL mode, or in BASIC using PRINT statements, for example,
Print a
Even if you execute it, the number of the pseudo part shows only up to 10 digits, and the numbers in the range of 0 and ±1 × 10 -99 to ±9.99,999,999 × 10 99 seem to be the limit, The numbers of FX-870P / VX-4 are expressed and calculated internally in BCD with 13 digits, index part 2 digits (0 and ±1 × 10 -99 ~ ±9.99,999,999,999 × 10 99 ) in BCD, The variables are also preserved with precision.
For example,
A=1.123456789012
Enter and enter,

and again,
